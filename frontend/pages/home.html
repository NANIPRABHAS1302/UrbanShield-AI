Predictive AI Pipeline Stack

Four coordinated AI modules ingest telemetry and calculate real-time safe vectors:

01 • CNN Computer Vision Module

Infers camera anomaly indexes, motion stability, and lighting quality from mock CCTV telemetry.

02 • GNN NetworkX Graph Router

Computes route metrics over city topology, weighting edges by distance, crime history, and lighting scores.

03 • Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL)

Simulates patrol mitigation vectors (patrols, crowd density flow) to calculate residual travel risk.

04 • Weighted Pydantic Risk Aggregator

Combines GNN, CNN, and MARL outputs with historical records to assign final safety bands (Low to Critical).
